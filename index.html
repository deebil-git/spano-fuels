About Spano Fuels

Your trusted fuel supply partner in the UAE

Reliable, Efficient, Professional

Spano Fuel Supply Services LLC, established in 2020, is one of the UAE’s leading distributors of diesel, fuel, oil, and petroleum products. We specialize in bulk fuel delivery, on‑site equipment fueling, generator fueling, and marine bunker services—serving construction, industrial, shipping, and heavy machinery sectors across the UAE.

Backed by an experienced operations team and a robust fleet, we ensure safe handling, quality assurance, compliant documentation, and on‑time delivery—24/7. With a monthly capacity of over 800,000 gallons, we scale with your demand while maintaining precision and reliability.
